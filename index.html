Beautiful woven jewellery

Whether you are looking for jewellery for that special occasion or simply a touch of everyday glamour, you'll find something special in the beautiful Beadart collection.

Each piece of intricate woven beadwork is delicately handmade by Gill Teece, with something to suit all tastes. Gill is also happy to discuss tailoring individual designs to order, should you want to match a particular colour or style.

Gill will be exhibiting her Beadart collection at forthcoming fairs around Shropshire, see the adjacent list of events. You can also find her work at the Ironbridge Gorge Museum Shop at Coalport China Works in Coalport, Shropshire. You can also contact her directly.
